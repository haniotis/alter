We

empower local leaders. expand frontiers. challenge conventional wisdom. find the world’s most promising ventures. build with the best. transform communities.

Find the best entrepreneurs in frontier markets

Every nation has a few game-changing entrepreneurs that create outsized value. We select the highest character, highest competency management teams leading the most scalable ventures.

Connect them to Silicon Valley Resources

Even the best entrepreneurs in frontier markets lack access to the resources they need to scale. We match them with talent, networks, expertise, and investments from the world’s most prolific entrepreneurial ecosystem - Silicon Valley.

Create impact at scale

Our ventures scale-up to create thousands of new jobs and deliver essential products and services to millions of people. Our entrepreneurs become role-models that lead the transformation of their own national ecosystems.

Where we work

Myanmar

Leapfrogging Tech Innovation

Political and economic reforms have turned Myanmar into one of the fastest growing economies in Asia. We see limitless potential in the country’s educated workforce that is dedicated to making good on the promise of newly opened markets. Myanmar is primed for the digital age.

Haiti

Liberation from Aid

Where foreign aid is failing, Alter’s entrepreneurs are successfully creating jobs and transforming their communities. Haiti boasts vast pools of talent and natural resources. We believe the right ventures can help pull the country’s economy away from cyclical poverty and reliance on outside assistance.

Afghanistan

Peace Through Entrepreneurship

Many outside observers see endless corruption and violence. We dug deeper and found an elite tier of Afghans leading businesses that buck the trend. They don’t rely on foreign aid. They refuse to bribe. And they turn strong profits. Alter’s entrepreneurs reach nation-wide to employ and empower their fellow citizens in this resource-rich nation.

Cuba

Building Partnerships for Prosperity

We’re on the ground right now, finding and selecting the best entrepreneurial talent Cuba has to offer. We’re excited to show off our great new partner companies soon.

Our Team

Born out of the Stanford Graduate School of Business, we live and work where Silicon Valley meets the frontier

Jesse Sullivan

Co-Founder/CEO

At a young age, I made a promise to devote my life to serving children dying of basic needs. I grew frustrated by my failed attempts to create change in the world’s toughest places through traditional charity, military, and government solutions. Alter was born out of a desire to bring together two radically different worlds: Silicon Valley, where the future is being created, and frontier markets, where many still struggle to survive.

Ozair Ali

Co-Founder/COO

I love solving puzzles - especially those with a purpose. The elimination of poverty is the puzzle I want to solve in my lifetime. I was born in a middle-class family in a developing country, but have been exceptionally fortunate to go to some of the best schools, and work across four continents in the public and private sector. With all of that, if I don't solve this puzzle, well, who will?
